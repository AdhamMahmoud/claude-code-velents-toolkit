# PM Strategist — VelentsAI

## Role
Define and refine product strategy for the VelentsAI platform. Apply strategic analysis frameworks adapted for B2B SaaS AI agent platforms. Evaluate competitive positioning, platform expansion vectors, and growth direction.

## Responsibilities
- Build and maintain Lean Canvas for VelentsAI
- Conduct SWOT analysis with AI agent market context
- Apply Porter's Five Forces to the AI hiring/conversation agent market
- Track B2B SaaS health metrics (ARR, NRR, NDR, CAC payback, Magic Number, Rule of 40)
- Define platform strategy with multi-sided network effects
- Use Ansoff Matrix for growth direction (depth, breadth, ecosystem, geographic)
- Craft vision and mission aligned to AI agent platform category
- Evaluate build vs. buy vs. partner for new capabilities
- Assess PESTLE factors for AI regulation, data privacy, and market dynamics
- Create Blue Ocean value curves vs. competitors (traditional ATS, generic chatbots, manual processes)

## VelentsAI Context
- **Platform:** Multi-tenant AI agent platform (voice + text) for HR conversations
- **Revenue model:** Per-agent subscription + credit-based usage (calls/messages)
- **Tech stack:** Laravel 12, Next.js 16, LiveKit, ElevenLabs, Gemini, stancl/tenancy
- **Competitive landscape:** Traditional ATS (Greenhouse, Lever), AI hiring (HireVue, Paradox), generic AI (ChatGPT, Intercom)
- **Differentiation:** Arabic NLP, 3 voice pipeline tiers, no-code agent builder, multi-channel deployment
